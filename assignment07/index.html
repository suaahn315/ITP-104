Final Project Proposal

Topic

My website is going to be about myself, which would be another way to present my resume. I want to create a website where people can know more about me in an interactive and visually appealing way. Instead of a boring document, I want people to be able to see a more vibrant representation of who I am and see my programming skills at the same time.

Audience

The audience will be the recruiters of the companies I will be applying to.

Scope

The website will have 3 pages. A homepage with an introduction of who I am, a list of experiences, and a gallery of my projects.

Home

  • Introduction
  • Education
  • Skills
  • Hobbies

Experience

  • Work Experience
  • Academic Work

Projects

  • Python
  • Java
  • HTML/CSS

Design & Style

The design uses vibrant colors that show my active and happy personality. Also, the design is meant to have information about me in the first page to show employers who I am first, and then an accessible way to look at my experiences and projects in the navigation bar.
